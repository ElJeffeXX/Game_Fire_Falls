Fire Fall

How to Play

Controls

  • WASD / Arrow keys move your clover around the battlefield.
  • Click to fire the beam from your orb.
  • Double-tap a direction to dash quickly.
  • Skip or choose upgrades between rounds to stay alive.

Health

Your health is split into two bars:

  • Player HP: your own life. If the green bar reaches 0, you lose.
  • World HP: the realm's life. When fire reaches your forest and the red bar reaches 0, the world is destroyed.

Upgrades can heal both you and the world, but only you will ever lose HP from enemy hits.

Upgrades

Between rounds you pick cards that boost your abilities. Choose carefully to shape your clover’s strengths.

  • Upgrades can increase damage, range, speed, dash cooldown, or health.
  • Some upgrades come with a tradeoff, like less range, slower speed, or reduced health.
  • Health upgrades heal both you and the world, while negative HP effects only hurt your player.
  • When you pick a card, it becomes stronger and the other choices disappear.

Player & Orb

Your clover controls the orb. When the orb shoots, it creates a glowing beam from its center.

  • The orb sits near you and visually charges as it fires.
  • Keep moving while shooting to dodge incoming enemies.
  • The orb and beam look sharp and bright — they are your core power.
